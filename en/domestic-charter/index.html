Domestic Shipping

Coastal Cargo Transportation

In recent years, maritime transport has played a vital role as an efficient means for mass and long-distance cargo transportation.
We are engaged in domestic coastal transport of liquid fuels and other cargoes to various places in Japan, including energy deliveries to the Tokyo Islands.

Ship Management

We consistently handle the operation, maintenance, and proper crew placement of our vessels—essential elements of maritime logistics—in close cooperation with our affiliated management company, Sanpo Shipping.
Our efforts support the safe and efficient operation of our owned vessels.

Future Outlook

Despite steady demand for domestic marine cargo transport, vessel availability and carrying capacity are declining.
We aim to contribute to the growth of the domestic maritime industry through strong collaboration with management companies and seafarers.

Commitment to Safety

We maintain close communication with charterers and management partners to constantly assess and ensure vessel safety.
With a strong focus on 'safety first,' we remain committed to reliable operations on every voyage.
